# Privacy and Data Protection Policy

**Effective Date: 2026-03-31**  

## Introduction  
This Privacy and Data Protection Policy outlines how we, at Newtown Kitchen, collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## Information We Collect  
We collect a variety of personal information to provide our services, including:  
- Identifiable Information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)  
- Health Information (e.g., medical history, treatment details)  
- Payment Information  

## How We Use Your Information  
Your information is used for:  
- Providing and managing our services  
- Communicating with you  
- Ensuring compliance with legal obligations  
- Promoting our services  

## Legal Basis for Processing Personal Information  
Under GDPR, our legal bases for processing personal information include:  
- Consent  
- Legal obligation  
- Legitimate interests  

## HIPAA Compliance  
We comply with HIPAA regulations by ensuring the security and confidentiality of Protected Health Information (PHI). We implement safeguards to protect your information and limit access on a need-to-know basis.

## Your Rights Under GDPR  
If you are a resident of the EU, you have the following rights:  
- The right to access your personal information  
- The right to rectification  
- The right to erasure  
- The right to restrict processing  
- The right to data portability  

## California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)  
Under CCPA, California residents have the right to:  
- Know what personal data is collected  
- Request deletion of personal data  
- Opt-out of the sale of personal data  

## Information Sharing and Disclosure  
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third parties only as necessary for service provision or as required by law.

## Security of Your Information  
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy and Data Protection Policy occasionally. We encourage you to review the policy periodically for any changes.

## Contact Us  
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@newtownkitchen.com.
